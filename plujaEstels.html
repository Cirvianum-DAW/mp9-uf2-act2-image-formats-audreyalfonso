What does Lighthouse tell about the image related perfomance of your pages?

Lighthouse identifies several issues related to image performance on the page, including potential improvements in image sizing, the elimination of render-blocking resources, and the suggestion to serve images in more efficient formats.

What are the main issues?

- Largest Contentful Paint (LCP): The LCP is exceptionally high at 78.7 s, indicating a significant delay in loading the largest content element.
- Image Sizing: Images could be better sized to reduce potential savings of 91,407 KiB.
- Render-Blocking Resources: There are render-blocking resources affecting the initial load time.

What are the main recommendations?:

- Largest Contentful Paint (LCP): Address the extremely high LCP time to improve the overall loading experience.
- Image Sizing: Properly size images to reduce the overall page size significantly.
- Render-Blocking Resources: Eliminate or defer render-blocking resources for a faster initial load time.
